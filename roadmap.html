Trust & Transparency

Measurable milestones, zero hype

Many modern open-source initiatives fall victim to making grand claims before code is even written. QXS Foundations maintains credibility by strictly delineating stages of technical readiness.

By defining our goals in transparent progress states, our community and research partners know exactly where our engineering blocks stand, allowing them to collaborate with realistic expectations.

Timeline Model

Pipeline Definitions

  • Research: Academic reviews and architectural drafts.
  • Prototype: Compilable proof of concepts on limited stacks.
  • Alpha: Full structural codebases made open for developer reviews.
  • Beta: Publicly tested platforms undergoing security audit remediation.
  • Release: Stable, supply-chain verified final builds.
