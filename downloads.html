Resilient Distribution

Resilient, secure, and decentralized

Corporate dependency starts with where you fetch your software. Relying on single-point centralized package indexes leaves developers exposed to supply-chain attacks, server downtime, and arbitrary repository deletions.

Our upcoming download portal (downloads.qxs-foundations.org.in) will use highly resilient, distributed mirror pathways to deliver cryptographically signed assets directly to developers — ensuring sovereign access under any operational condition.
